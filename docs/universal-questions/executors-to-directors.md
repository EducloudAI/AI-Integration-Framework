# üß† How Universal Questions Enable the Shift  
Universal questions guide people to think like directors:

### **1. They encourage decision making**  
What is the goal  
What is the impact  
What is the right direction  

### **2. They strengthen judgment**  
What are the risks  
What defines quality  
What requires human oversight  

### **3. They build ownership**  
Who is responsible  
What needs improvement  
What must be validated  

### **4. They support strategic thinking**  
How does this create value  
Which KPI does this influence  
How will we measure success  

### **5. They develop leadership**  
What should we prioritize  
How do we align perspectives  
What direction should we take  

This is the mindset of a director, not an executor.
