# Quick Start Guide: AI Prompt Framework

## üöÄ Get Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Choose Your Template
- **For Business Teams:** Use `prompt-framework-template.xlsx`
- **For Developers:** Use `prompt-definition.json`
- **For Quick Testing:** Use this guide

### Step 2: Fill Tier 1 Questions (Essential)
Start with these 3 questions for any AI task:

1. **Task Description:** What should AI do?
   *Example:* "Generate weekly sales report summaries"

2. **Intended Use:** Where/when will this be used?
   *Example:* "Monday morning management meetings"

3. **Quality Criteria:** What makes a good output?
   *Example:* "Accurate numbers, clear insights, under 500 words"

### Step 3: Add Context & Constraints
- **Context:** Background information
- **Constraints:** Limits and boundaries
- **Human Oversight:** Who reviews the output

### Step 4: Test & Iterate
1. Use your prompt with ChatGPT/Copilot/Gemini
2. Review the output
3. Refine your questions
4. Repeat until satisfied

## üìä Tier 1 vs Tier 2: When to Use What?

### Tier 1 Only (10 Questions)
- Daily tasks
- Non-critical processes
- Internal use only
- Low-risk applications

### Tier 1 + Tier 2 (25 Questions)
- Customer-facing applications
- Regulated industries (finance, healthcare)
- High-stakes decisions
- EU AI Act compliance required
